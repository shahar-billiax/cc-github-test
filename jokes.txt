Developer Jokes

1. Why do programmers prefer dark mode?
   Because light attracts bugs!

2. A SQL query walks into a bar, walks up to two tables and asks...
   "Can I join you?"

3. How many programmers does it take to change a light bulb?
   None, that's a hardware problem!

4. Why do Java developers wear glasses?
   Because they don't C#!

5. What's a programmer's favorite hangout place?
   Foo Bar!

6. Why did the developer go broke?
   Because he used up all his cache!

7. A programmer's wife tells him: "Run to the store and pick up a loaf of bread. If they have eggs, get a dozen."
   The programmer comes home with 12 loaves of bread.
